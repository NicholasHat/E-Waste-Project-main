A Sustainable Future
In a time of rapid advancements in technology, there is an unprecedented increase in electronic waste, which poses significant risks to the climate and human health if improperly disposed of. To take steps towards saving our environment we need to tackle this growing issue. We need new policies to prevent unsafe and improper disposal of e-waste (electronic waste) through common recycling regulations in place for littering.


Implementing Reward System and Harsher Fines

To back e-waste disposal regulations, new policies could introduce harsher, and more specific fines for businesses and individuals who fail to follow the regulation, to make sure there is a consistent punishment. Fines could get more severe as mistakes are repeated. But also a reward system for disposing of e-waste properly. The government could fund recycling centers to provide monetary rewards for people who give their old devices to these centers.


Creating and enforcing policies for proper e-waste disposal is crucial in taking steps to manage our e-waste problem and a big first step in fighting climate change together. As the world embraces technological advancements we must first prioritize keeping our environment clean and safe, for a brighter future.

A Sustainable Future
In a time of rapid advancements in technology, there is an unprecedented increase of electronic waste, which has significant risks to the climate and human health if improperly disposed of. In order to take steps towards saving our environment we need to tackle this growing issue. We need new policies to prevent unsafe and improper disposal of e-waste (electronic waste) through common recycling regulations in place for littering.


Implementing Fines and Penalties

To back e-waste disposal regulations, new policies could introduce fines towards businesses and individuals that fail to follow the regulations. Just like a fine for littering, when you are seen throwing away e-waste improperly you will be fined by local law enforcement or a new department focused towards proper e-waste management. And businesses who improperly dispose of e-waste could be punished and regulated by the EPA in the US or other environmental protection agencies, and failure to meet the guidelines could result in large fines and public disclosure.


Creating and enforcing policy for proper e-waste disposal is crucial in taking steps to manage our e-waste problem and a big first step in fighting climate change together. As the world embraces technological advancements we must first prioritize keeping our environment clean and safe, for a brighter future.

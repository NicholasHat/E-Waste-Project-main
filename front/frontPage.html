BOOST LONGEVITY

It's extremely important to take measures to boost the longevity of your devices -- not only for your wallet, but also the environment. Regularly updating your software and cleaning parts of your computer can help increase the lifespan of your device. This will reduce the need to buy and create new computers meaning less resources need to be extracted for the environment. The longer you hold onto your device, the less e-waste you create!

CHOOSE RECYCLING

Recycling is always the better option! Throwing away e-waste poses significant risks to the environment. When e-waste is discarded it can release hazardous substances including lead and mercury into the soil and groundwater. These substances are extremely toxic and can be hazardous if ingested. Furthermore, electronic devices require valuable materials such as gold, and copper. Recycling ensures that we can conserve these materials reducing the need to mine more of it -- which saves precious ecosystems. Not to mention that recycling minimizes the energy and carboonprint needed to produce entirely new technological products. By recycling you can help make the environment a cleaner and healthier place!
